# Tzaangor Enlightened

**Role:** Other

*Blessed amongst their mutated kind, the Enlightened can see the skeins of fate through communion with Tzeentch. They surge atop daemonic Discs on the hunt for esoteric knowledge. Tzaangor Enlightened close on their targets, emitting screeches of anticipatory triumph, and their divining spears hum as they sense their predestined prey.*
