# Wraithguard

**Role:** Other

*Wraithguard are all but impervious to pain. This is vitally important, for their D-scythes and wraithcannons are short-ranged, and require them to advance close to the foe. These immensely powerful weapons fire arcane beams that open a momentary rift between realspace and the warp, capable of tearing any target apart.*
