# Legionaries

**Role:** Battleline

*Against the onslaught of Chaos Legionaries, few can stand for long. Whether millennia-old veterans of the Long War or traitors from renegade warbands, Legionaries are deadly and adaptable warriors, unleashing streams of explosive bolts or venting their vicious hatred with swings of rending chainswords.*

**Abilities:**
- **Dark Pacts** (Faction): If your Army Faction is HERETIC ASTARTES, each time a unit with  this ability is selected to shoot or fight, it can make a Dark Pact. If it does, it must first take a Leadership test before any effects of that Dark Pact are resolved; if that test is failed, that unit suffers D3 mortal wounds. Then, select one of the following abilities for that unit’s weapons to gain until the end of the phase:
- [LETHAL HITS]
- [SUSTAINED HITS 1]
- **Veterans of the Long War** (Datasheet): Each time a model in this unit targets an enemy unit with a melee attack, re-roll a Wound roll of 1. If that enemy unit is within range of an objective marker, you can re-roll the Wound roll instead.
- **Chaos Icon** (Wargear): Each time the bearer’s unit takes a Leadership test for the Dark Pacts ability, you can re-roll that test.
