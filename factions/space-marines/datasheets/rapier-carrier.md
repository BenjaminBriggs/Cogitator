# Rapier Carrier

**Role:** Other

*The Rapier Armoured Carrier is a bulky, tracked device that traces its origin to the dawn of Mankind’s stellar empire. Compatible with various heavy weapons, the Rapier is most commonly fitted with a powerful quad lascannon known as a laser destroyer, making it a compact but potent anti-armour asset.*

- You can re-roll the Hit roll.
