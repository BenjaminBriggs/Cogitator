# Invader ATV

**Role:** Other

*The Invader is a highly flexible all-terrain vehicle, perfectly adapted to an aggressive reconnaissance role. Outfitted with either a multi-melta or onslaught gatling cannon, it can rapidly deliver punishing fire against vulnerable parts of the enemy line, or swiftly engage and destroy scouting elements of opposing forces.*

**Abilities:**
- **Oath of Moment** (Faction): If your Army Faction is ADEPTUS ASTARTES, at the start of your Command phase, select one unit from your opponent’s army. Until the start of your next Command phase, that enemy unit is your Oath of Moment target. Each time a model with this ability makes an attack that targets your Oath of Moment target:
- You can re-roll the Hit roll.
- If you are using a *Codex: Space Marines* Detachment and your army does not include one or more units with the Black Templars, Blood Angels, Dark Angels, Deathwatch or Space Wolves keywords, add 1 to the Wound roll as well.
- **Outrider Escort** (Datasheet): Once per turn, in your opponent’s Shooting phase, when another friendly ADEPTUS ASTARTES MOUNTED unit within 6" of this model is selected as the target of an attack, one model from your army with this ability can use it. If it does, after that enemy unit has finished making its attacks, that model can shoot as if it were your Shooting phase, but when resolving those attacks it can only target that enemy unit (and only if it is an eligible target).
