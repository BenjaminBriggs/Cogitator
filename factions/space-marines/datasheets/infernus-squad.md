# Infernus Squad

**Role:** Other

*Infernus Squads purge swathes of the enemy ranks with the incandescent firestorms they unleash from their pyreblasters. They are close assault specialists, sending jets of burning promethium into enemy trench lines and bunkers and through dense ruins and concealing vegetation, ensuring no foe escapes their fiery wrath.*

**Abilities:**
- **Oath of Moment** (Faction): If your Army Faction is ADEPTUS ASTARTES, at the start of your Command phase, select one unit from your opponent’s army. Until the start of your next Command phase, that enemy unit is your Oath of Moment target. Each time a model with this ability makes an attack that targets your Oath of Moment target:
- You can re-roll the Hit roll.
- If you are using a *Codex: Space Marines* Detachment and your army does not include one or more units with the Black Templars, Blood Angels, Dark Angels, Deathwatch or Space Wolves keywords, add 1 to the Wound roll as well.
- **Incendiary Terror** (Datasheet): In your Shooting phase, after this unit has shot, you can select one enemy INFANTRY unit hit by one or more of those attacks made with a pyreblaster. That enemy unit must take a Battle-shock test, subtracting 1 from that test.
