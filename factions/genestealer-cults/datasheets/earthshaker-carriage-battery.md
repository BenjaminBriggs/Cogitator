**Abilities:**
- **Earthshaker Rounds** (Datasheet): In your Shooting phase, after this model has shot, if one or more of those attacks made with its earthshaker cannon scored a hit against an INFANTRY unit, until the end of your opponent’s next turn, that INFANTRY unit is shaken. While a unit is shaken, subtract 2" from its Move characteristic and subtract 2 from Advance and Charge rolls made for it.
