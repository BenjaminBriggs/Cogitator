**Abilities:**
- **Final Duty** (Datasheet): While the Fire Coordinator model is on the battlefield, each time a Heavy Weapons Gunner model is destroyed, roll one D6: on a 3+, do not remove it from play. The destroyed model can shoot after the attacking model’s unit has finished making its attacks, and is then removed from play.
- **EMBARKING** (Special (правая колонка)): While embarked within a Transport, each Heavy Weapons Gunner model takes up the space of 2 models, and each weapon equipped by these models is considered to be 2 models’ weapons for the purposes of the Firing Deck ability.
