**Abilities:**
- **Oath of Moment** (Faction): If your Army Faction is ADEPTUS ASTARTES, at the start of your Command phase, select one unit from your opponent’s army. Until the start of your next Command phase, that enemy unit is your Oath of Moment target. Each time a model with this ability makes an attack that targets your Oath of Moment target:
- You can re-roll the Hit roll.
- If you are using a *Codex: Space Marines* Detachment and your army does not include one or more units with the Black Templars, Blood Angels, Dark Angels, Deathwatch or Space Wolves keywords, add 1 to the Wound roll as well.
- **Annihilator Protocols** (Datasheet): Melee weapons equipped by models in this unit have the [SUSTAINED HITS 2] ability when targeting MONSTER, VEHICLE or FORTIFICATION units.
- **Centurion Assault Launcher** (Wargear): The bearer has the Grenades keyword.

**Keywords:** Infantry, Imperium, Centurion, Centurion Assault Squad
**Faction Keywords:** Adeptus Astartes

**Unit Composition:**
- 1 Assault Centurion Sergeant
- 2-5 Assault Centurions

**Wargear Options:**
- Any number of models can each have their twin flamer replaced with 1 twin meltagun.
- Any number of models can each have their Centurion bolters replaced with 1 Centurion assault launcher.
