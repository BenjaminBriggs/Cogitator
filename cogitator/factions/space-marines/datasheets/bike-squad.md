# Bike Squad

**Role:** Other

*Deployed in rapid assault missions based on intelligence gathered by Scouts and Vanguard forces, Bikers can smash through enemy lines, battlefield obstacles and even ferrocrete walls due to the raw fury of their mounts’ engines. Their sheer bulk belies their phenomenal power, speed and manoeuvrability.*

**Abilities:**
- **Oath of Moment** (Faction): If your Army Faction is ADEPTUS ASTARTES, at the start of your Command phase, select one unit from your opponent’s army. Until the start of your next Command phase, that enemy unit is your Oath of Moment target. Each time a model with this ability makes an attack that targets your Oath of Moment target:
- You can re-roll the Hit roll.
- If you are using a *Codex: Space Marines* Detachment and your army does not include one or more units with the Black Templars, Blood Angels, Dark Angels, Deathwatch or Space Wolves keywords, add 1 to the Wound roll as well.
- **Turbo-boost** (Datasheet): Each time this unit Advances, do not make an Advance roll for it. Instead, until the end of the phase, add 6" to the Move characteristic of models in this unit.
- **ATTACHED UNITS** (Special (правая колонка)): If a Character unit from your army with the Leader ability can be attached to an Outrider Squad, it can also be attached to this unit.
